How It Works

Three Simple Steps

1. Get Verified

A local business securely puts its registration on the platform, creating a trusted digital identity everyone can see.

2. Create Shares

Based on fair and transparent data (like tax returns), the business creates digital shares for the community to buy.

3. Trade Locally

A simple marketplace where you can browse, buy, and sell shares in the local businesses you believe in.
