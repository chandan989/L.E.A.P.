Core Protocol Architecture

Three Phases to On-Chain Syndication

Phase I: Encode

An enterprise's legal data is encoded into a unique "Syndicate NFT," creating an immutable proof-of-existence.

Phase II: Synthesize

Verified data streams are used to calibrate a fair valuation, synthesizing equity into fungible "Equity Access Tokens" (EATs).

Phase III: Exchange

EATs are listed on the L.E.A.P. Terminal, allowing investors to acquire, trade, and provide liquidity in a 24/7 market.
